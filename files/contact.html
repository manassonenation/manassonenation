Frequently Asked Questions

How can I become a member of Manass One Nation?

Becoming a member is easy! You can visit our community center during business hours to complete a registration form, or you can register online through our website. We offer different membership levels to suit your needs and budget.

What facilities are available at the community center?

Our community center features a fully-equipped gym, sports courts (basketball, volleyball), a swimming pool, meeting rooms, a co-working space, and a caf√©. We also have dedicated spaces for classes and workshops.

Do you offer programs for children and youth?

Yes! We have a variety of programs designed specifically for children and youth, including after-school sports programs, summer camps, leadership workshops, and educational activities. All our youth programs are supervised by qualified staff.

How can I book a facility for a private event?

To book any of our facilities for private events (birthday parties, corporate events, meetings), please contact our events coordinator at events@manassonenationkadunaregus@gmail.com or call +2347063849950 +2349041356195 +2348144722911 at least two weeks in advance.
